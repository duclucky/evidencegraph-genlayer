## Capture notes

- Record at 1920×1080 or 1280×720, 30 fps.
- Use smooth cursor movement and 400–600 ms pauses after clicks.
- Keep browser zoom at 100% and hide bookmarks or personal browser data.
- Add captions in editing; no narration or music is required.
- Never show real credentials, private URLs, wallet data, or transaction secrets.
